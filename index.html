Musical Instruments of the World


A presentation of various musical instruments and their classification across different cultures. In some cases, instruments remain in wide use within the nation but in others, their importance is primarily symbolic. Each instrument is also classified by the Hornbostel-Sachs system in which they're categorized based on the sound they produce.
